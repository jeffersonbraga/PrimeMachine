Any doubts?

Which social networks does Woop support?

We support Facebook Profiles, Facebook Pages, Instagram, Twitter, Linkedin Profiles and Linkedin Pages.

how does the freemium plan works?

When using our freemium tools, you get full access to our features, with one exception: the advanced reports modules are only available when purchasing a plan. You may add one social channel for each network and post as much as you please, with no further limitations.

What does social channel stand for?

Each social channel of any social network supported by Woop is count as 1. For example, 8 Facebook pages, 5 Instagram profiles and 2 Twitter profiles would count as 15 social channels in total.

How can I upgrade to add more social channels or users?

You can purchase channels on our custom plan. We feel/think/prefer that you pay only for what suits you. Each new extra profile costs 1 dollars/month and at every 5 profiles you get one new team member license.

What are the main differences between each plan?

The two differences between the plans are on how many social profiles you may add and how many team members can work on the same account. You have full access to all our features in all plans.

How long does the contract last?

You can pay month-by-month, or annually. It is possible to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time, with no extra charges. Please note that we do not refund downgrades or cancellation. given billing cycle only.

Can I cancel anytime?

You may cancel your account anytime and you will be charged for the given billing cycle only.

Do you offer a discount for digital influencers?

Yes! Write an honest review of Woop on your blog and we'll give you 6 months free on our 1st plan.

CAN I PAY ANNUALLY?

Yes, and it's worth it! A full year's subscription saves you 15% compared to paying monthly. You can upgrade from monthly to yearly at any time.

ARE THERE ANY HIDDEN FEES?

Our prices are all-inclusive. There are no setup or cancellation fees, and no additional fees or taxes.

What are my payment options?

We accept all major credit cards. Mostly, payments are taken monthly but we also offer a 15% discount if you choose to pay for a full year.

DO YOU OFFER special discounts or plans for students, researchers or charity?

Registered charities, nonprofits, and students in full-time education may use all of our features for free. Get in touch!

Do you offer free trial?

If you want to test our features, you can start using the Freemium plan, where all resources are available, including a week with full access to the reports.
